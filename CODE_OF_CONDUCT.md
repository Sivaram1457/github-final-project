# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability,
ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the
standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public
spaces when an individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may
be reported to the project maintainers.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Project maintainers will follow and enforce these guidelines fairly and
consistently.
